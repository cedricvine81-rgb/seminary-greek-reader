# Student Workflow

## 1. Create an Account

Go to `/auth/sign-up`, select **Student**, fill in your details.

## 2. Access the Greek Reader

The Greek Reader at `/reader` is available to all users (no login required). You can:
- Toggle between the Greek New Testament and LXX
- Navigate by book and chapter
- Search by Greek word or biblical reference
- Hover any Greek word to see its parsing and gloss

## 3. Your Dashboard

After signing in, you are taken to `/student`. The dashboard shows:
- Enrolled courses
- Pending and completed assignments
- Average score

## 4. Complete Assignments

Go to **Assignments** to see your list. Click an assignment to begin.

**Vocabulary Quizzes** — multiple-choice questions, auto-graded immediately.

**Morphology Quizzes** — use dropdown menus to select:
case, number, gender, tense, voice, mood, person, etc.

**Translation Exercises** — type your translation and use dropdowns to identify syntax categories.

## 5. Study with Flashcards

Go to **Flashcards** to study Greek vocabulary with spaced repetition.

- Choose Beginning Greek (50+ frequency) or Intermediate Greek (30+ frequency)
- Click the card to reveal the answer
- Press **Know It** or **Don't Know** — the app schedules your next review using the SM-2 algorithm

## 6. Track Progress

**Progress** page: see your completion rate, average score, and accuracy broken down by assignment type.

**Scores** page: full history of completed assignments with scores.
